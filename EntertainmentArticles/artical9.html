Kisi Ka Bhai… back with another Telugu song; Ram Charan’s cameo wins hearts

Starring Salman Khan, Pooja Hegde and Venkatesh in lead roles, the film is slated for release on the occasion of Eid

By Bhavana Published on 4 April 2023 5:06 PM

Hyderabad: The Bathukamma song from ‘Kisi Ka Bhai Kisi Ki Jaan’ has taken the internet by storm. Starring Salman Khan, Pooja Hegde, and Venkatesh, the film is slated for release on 21 April, on the occasion of Eid.

After the Bathukamma song went viral, the makers released another song from the film and again, it has some Telugu verses that make the song special.

‘RRR’ star Ram Charan appears glowing and energetic in this euphoric song that also features Bollywood superstar Salman Khan, Victory Venkatesh, and Pooja Hegde. The Mega Power Star makes a stylish entry in a shimmering yellow shirt and white lungi. He shakes a leg with his real-life friend Salman and Tollywood star Venkatesh. The choreography adds to the charm of the dance moves of the ‘RRR’ star, whose ease and style quotient are graceful.

The lead actors dance wearing lungis and their moves are making fans go gaga. Watching Salman ace the south Indian avatar and reciting dialogues is worth watching.

When the film hits the screens this Eid, the ‘Game Changer’ actor’s fans will surely be in for a treat. The song is composed by Payal Dev.

Also, it is quite clear that the Bollywood stars are trying to make the best of their southern stars and their rapport with them. Salman Khan was seen playing an extended cameo in Chiranjeevi’s ‘Godfather’ and now, he has returned the favour with Charan’s cameo in this song.
