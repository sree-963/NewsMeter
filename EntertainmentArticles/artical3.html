Happy Birthday Allu Arjun: Some interesting facts about the Pushpa star

Over the years, the stylish star has left an impeccable mark in the south film industry

By Bhavana Published on 8 April 2023 1:48 PM

Hyderabad: Allu Arjun, fondly known as Bunny by his fans, is a huge star in south India. Over the years, the stylish star has left an impeccable mark in the South film industry. Be it any role, his fans always look forward to his movies. The actor’s last outing, Pushpa: The Rise, was a roaring success at the worldwide box office which bought him craze across the globe. Let’s look at some lesser-known noteworthy facts about the actor.

Debut

Though Allu Arjun made his debut as a hero with Raghavendra Rao’s directorial Gangotri in 2003, he was actually a Tollywood child artist and made his debut in the 1985 movie Vijetha. He was only 4 years old at the time. He also acted in Swathi Muthyam (1986), and in Chiranjeevi’s Daddy (2001) as a dancer.

His all-time favourite movie

Allu Arjun’s all-time favourite movie is Chiranjeevi’s Indra. In an interview, he said that he has watched that movie over 15 times and continues to do so when he has time.

Bunny enjoys a luxurious lifestyle

The actor is a huge fan of driving and cars. He has a huge list of luxury car collections and his most expensive purchases include Hummer H2, Range Rover Vogue, Jaguar XJL, and Volvo XC90 T8. The actor also has a stunning vanity van that costs around Rs 7 crores. He named it Falcon and the fabulous black vanity van is a head-turner on any film set. Besides this, he also has a private jet.

Telugu star has a huge fan following in Kerala

He is the only Telugu actor with a dedicated fan following in Kerala even before his Pushpa release. He has had a massive fan base there. His film Aarya was his claim to fame in this region. The dubbed version established Allu Arjun as a superstar in Kerala, at par with the reigning stars such as Prithviraj and Jayasurya.

Telugu blockbuster of all time

His film Ala Vaikunthapurramuloo is a massive hit at the box office and is among the top Telugu blockbusters of all time.

Hidden talents

Apart from being an extraordinary actor, he is a great dancer. He is best known for his style, grace, and impeccable dancing skills. His style of dancing was a unique blend of classical and contemporary dance forms, which set him apart from his contemporaries. He is a singer as well. In 2016, he lent his voice to a track in Sarrainodu.
