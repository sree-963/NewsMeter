Ustaad Bhagat Singh: Pawan Kalyan starrer goes on floors; makers share poster

The film officially went on floors yesterday and fans cannot keep their calm

By Bhavana Published on 7 April 2023 11:42 AM

Hyderabad: Pawan Kalyan's much-awaited film Ustaad Bhagat Singh has finally gone on floors. Directed by Harish Shankar, the film was announced a few years ago. It was postponed for quite a lot of reasons. This is one of the most-awaited films. Fans of Pawan Kalyan have huge expectations from the movie.

The film is bankrolled by Y Ravi Shankar and Naveen Yerneni under Mythri Movie Makers. The film officially went on floors yesterday and fans cannot keep their calm.

The first schedule of the film will start at a specially erected police station set over a week featuring Pawan Kalyan and other lead actors. Cinematographer Ayananka Bose, production designer Anand Sai, and director Harish Shankar extensively worked on the pre-production of the shoot and they’re confident of surpassing viewers’ expectations with an entertaining film.

A new poster of the film featuring Pawan Kalyan in a flamboyant avatar was unveiled by the makers and the star looks effortlessly stylish and at ease in this look.

Several big names have been finalized for Ustaad Bhagat Singh’s cast. Telugu cinema’s most happening heroine Sreeleela is also on board, while Ashutosh Rana, Nawab Shah, KGF fame Avinash, Gauthami, Narra Srinu, Naga Mahesh, and Temper Vamsi essay supporting roles.

Besides cinematographer Ayananka Bose and art director Anand Sai, the filmmakers have hired a top-notch technical team including editor Chota K Prasad. The noted music director who has composed tunes for Jalsa, Gabbar Singh, Attarintiki Daredi, Pushpa, and Rangasthalam, Devi Sri Prasad, has also been roped in.

Besides Ustaad Bhagat Singh, Pawan Kalyan has Hari Hara Veera Mallu and Vinodaya Sita Remake in his kitty. Above all, he is going to keep himself busy with politics as well.
