Christians did not pray at Warangal’s Thousand Pillar Temple

NewsMeter verified that the video is not from Thousand Pillar Temple, and the claim is false.

By Md Mahfooz Alam Published on 12 Apr 2023 10:45 AM

A video showing a group of people praying inside a pillared complex is going viral on social media.

Several Facebook users have claimed that the video shows Christians praying inside the Thousand Pillar Temple in Warangal.

Click here, here, here, here, here, here, and here to see more posts.

Former CBI Director M. Nageswara Rao shared the video claiming that the video could be from Thousand Pillar Temple or any other temple.

The official Twitter handle of the Commissioner of Police, Warangal, replying to Rao’s tweet, said that the incident took place at the Warangal Fort. It also mentioned that culprits were arrested and action will be taken against them for disturbing communal harmony. A criminal case has been registered at Mills Colony police station.

Telangana police chief also took to Twitter to confirm that the incident did not take place at Thousand Pillar Temple. He said Telangana remains committed to protecting the secular ethos and also warned that those spreading the videos will be questioned by the police.

Hence, we conclude that the video does not show people praying at the Thousand Pillar Temple. The claim is false.
