Heatwave grips Telangana: MeT says light rains may occur on April 15

Daytime temperature is likely to rise steadily and may reach 40°C in various parts of the state

By Newsmeter Network Published on 12 Apr 2023 11:37 AM

Hyderabad: A severe heatwave has gripped Telangana with mercury crossing 43.8°C at Dasturabad in Nirmal.

Hyderabad is also witnessing sweltering heat with Maitrivanam Ameerpet recording a temperature of 39.3°C. Temperature is likely to soar in the coming days.

According to the Indian Meteorological Department's Weather Analysis, winds are blowing from the southeast towards Telangana state at the lower level because of which the state is likely to have dry weather for the next three days.

Daytime temperature is likely to rise steadily and may reach 40°C (degrees Celsius) in various parts of the state.

Weather Forecast in Telangana for the next few days

April 12 to April 13: Telangana is expected to have dry weather. Maximum temperatures in scattered spots across Telangana are expected to be 2°C to 4°C above normal.

A mostly clear sky will give way to a partly cloudy sky later in the evening in most areas.

April 14: Telangana is expected to have dry weather. Maximum temperatures in scattered spots across Telangana are expected to be 2°C to 4°C above normal.

The sky will be partly cloudy in most areas. Thunderstorms are likely to form later in the evening or at night.

April 15: Mild to moderate rain or thundershowers are expected in isolated locations in Telangana. Maximum temperatures in scattered spots across Telangana are expected to be 2°C to 4°C above normal.

A partly cloudy sky. Thunderstorms are expected to form later in the evening or at night in isolated areas.
